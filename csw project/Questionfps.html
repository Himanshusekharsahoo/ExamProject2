Exam Form For PROBABILITY AND STATISTICS

1. If you roll a fair 6-sided die twice, what is the probability of getting at least one 5?


2. A bag contains 4 red marbles, 3 blue marbles, and 2 yellow marbles. You randomly draw two marbles without replacement. What is the probability of drawing one red marble and one blue marble?


3. The average height of a class of 20 students is 165 cm. If one student leaves the class, the average height decreases to 164 cm. What is the height of the student who left?


4. You flip a coin twice. What is the probability of getting heads on both flips?


5. A survey finds that 60% of people like dogs and 40% like cats. If 20% of people like both dogs and cats, what percentage of people like only dogs?
